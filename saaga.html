Colour Palette

Xbox Green (Primary)

#107C10
RGB: 16, 124, 16
Primary brand color for headings, buttons, and key interactive elements. Represents Xbox's core identity and visual recognition.

Dark Green (Secondary)

#054B16
RGB: 5, 75, 22
Secondary accent color for hover states, subheadings, and decorative elements. Complements primary green without overwhelming.

Light Gray (Neutral)

#E6E6E6
RGB: 230, 230, 230
Neutral background color for content areas, cards, and page sections. Ensures readability and visual breathing room.

Dark Gray (Neutral)

#737373
RGB: 115, 115, 115
Neutral text color for body content, captions, and secondary information. Avoids harsh contrast with light backgrounds.
